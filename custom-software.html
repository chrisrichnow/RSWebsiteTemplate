Questions

Straight answers

How much does custom software cost?

It scales with scope. A focused automation or internal tool is far less than a full custom platform. We scope clearly during discovery so you get a real number — and we can phase the build to fit your budget.

How long does it take?

Smaller tools can ship in a few weeks; larger platforms take longer and are built in phases. We always start with the highest-value piece so you get something working sooner rather than later.

Do you maintain it after launch?

Yes. Software needs upkeep and grows over time — we offer ongoing support and continued development so it keeps working as your needs change.

What technology do you build with?

Modern, proven, well-supported tools — and the latest AI models where they add real value. We choose the stack based on what's right for your project and what's maintainable long-term, not trends.
